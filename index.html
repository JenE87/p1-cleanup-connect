Cleanup & Connect

Who we are

... a growing group of neighbours, friends, and changemakers who care about the place we call home.
Cleanup & Connect started with a simple idea: to bring people together to take small, meaningful steps towards a cleaner environment and a stronger community. We believe that picking up litter is about more than just cleaning up. It's about sparking conversations, building relationships and fostering a shared sense of pride in our neighbourhoods.

Why join

For the Environment

Every bottle picked up and every wrapper removed helps restore the natural beauty of our parks, streets, and green spaces. Small acts of care add up to big impact.

For the Next Generation

By setting an example today, we teach children the value of responsibility, respect, and action. We’re not just cleaning - we’re creating a better world for those who follow us.

For your Community

Cleanup events bring people together across backgrounds and ages. Whether you meet a new neighbor or catch up with an old friend, you're strengthening the bond of your community.

For your Health

Fresh air, movement, and a sense of purpose - cleanup days are a win for your body and your mind. Giving back feels good in every way.
